About Book Finder

Our Mission

Book Finder is dedicated to helping readers discover their next great read. We provide access to millions of books through the Google Books API, offering detailed information and easy search capabilities.

Features

  • Search through millions of books
  • View detailed book information
  • Browse by categories and authors
  • Fast and responsive interface
  • Free to use

Technology

Built with Angular 18 and powered by Google Books API, this application demonstrates modern web development practices including routing, HTTP services, and component-based architecture.
